Monday: Upper Body Strength

  • Warm-up: 5 minutes dynamic stretches (arm circles, push-ups)
  • Floor Dumbbell Chest Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Standing Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Floor Dumbbell Flys (chest): 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Overhead Tricep Extension (triceps): 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Push-ups (bodyweight): 2 sets of max reps
  • Cool down: Stretch for chest, shoulders, and triceps
